## 🔒 Critical Safety Features

Version 1.6.0 introduces **Phase 1.5 Critical Safety Features** - a comprehensive suite of protections designed to safeguard your trading capital and provide emergency controls.

### Emergency Stop Button

**What it is:**
A new emergency stop button in the admin dashboard that immediately halts all trading activity and closes open positions.

**What you can do:**
- Stop all trading instantly with one click
- Close all open positions at market prices
- Cancel all pending orders
- Protect capital during unexpected market events

**How to use it:**
1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** (admin users only)
2. Scroll to the **Emergency Stop Controls** section
3. Click **Emergency Stop** for the bot you want to halt
4. Confirm the action in the dialog
5. Bot will stop trading and close positions within 100 milliseconds

**Important:**
- Only available to admin users
- Equity bot can only close positions during market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST)
- Crypto bot works 24/7 (crypto markets never close)
- Use only in genuine emergencies - this will close ALL positions

## 🛡️ Automated Capital Protection

### Bracket Orders (Behind the Scenes)

**What changed:**
Every trade now automatically includes stop-loss and take-profit orders placed at the same time as the entry order.

**What this means for you:**
- Your positions are protected even if the system crashes
- Stop-loss orders prevent catastrophic losses
- Take-profit orders automatically lock in gains
- No manual order management needed

**How it works:**
- **Equity bot:** Uses Alpaca's atomic bracket orders (all 3 orders in one request)
- **Crypto bot:** Places entry, stop-loss, and take-profit in rapid sequence (~1-2 seconds)

**Example:**
If you buy 100 shares of AAPL at $150:
- Stop-loss automatically placed at $145.50 (3% risk)
- Take-profit automatically placed at $157.50 (5% reward)
- Risk/reward ratio: 1.67:1

---

### Hard Position Limits (Behind the Scenes)

**What changed:**
Code-enforced limits now prevent excessive risk-taking.

**What this means for you:**
- Maximum 10% of portfolio in any single position
- Maximum 5 positions at one time
- Daily loss limit of 3% triggers circuit breaker
- Total portfolio risk capped at 15%
- Minimum position size: $100
- Maximum position size: $50,000

**Circuit Breaker:**
If daily losses reach 3%, the system automatically:
- Blocks all new trades until the next trading day
- Logs a critical alert
- Protects remaining capital

**Why this matters:**
These limits are **hard-coded** and cannot be changed via configuration. This prevents accidental or malicious changes that could expose your account to excessive risk.
