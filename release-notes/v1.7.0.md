# Version 1.7.0 - Critical Safety Features

**Release Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Type:** Minor Release  
**Focus:** Trading Safety & Reliability

---

## 🛡️ What's New

### Position Recovery System
Your trading bot now automatically syncs positions with your broker on every startup. If the bot crashes or you make manual trades, it will detect and reconcile any differences between the database and your actual broker positions.

**Benefits:**
- No more "ghost positions" in the database
- Automatic recovery from crashes
- Database always matches broker reality

---

### Graceful Strategy Failure Handling
Individual strategy failures no longer crash the entire bot. If one strategy encounters an error, the other strategies continue trading normally. After 3 consecutive failures, the problematic strategy is automatically disabled while the rest keep running.

**Benefits:**
- One broken strategy doesn't stop all trading
- Automatic recovery when issues resolve
- Full visibility via Prometheus metrics

---

### Crypto Bot Trading Fix
The crypto bot has been converted from futures to spot trading and can now execute trades properly. Account balances and positions are fetched correctly from Coinbase.

**Benefits:**
- Crypto bot can now open positions
- Proper spot trading support
- Better error handling and logging

---

### Atomic Performance Tracking
All position updates now use database transactions with row-level locking. This prevents race conditions and ensures your P&L calculations are always accurate, even if the bot crashes mid-update.

**Benefits:**
- No double-counting of profits/losses
- Crash-safe position updates
- Consistent data across all reads
