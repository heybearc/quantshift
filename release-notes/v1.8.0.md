# What's New in Version 1.8.0

**Released:** March 4, 2026

---

## ✨ New Features

### 📊 Market Regime Dashboard

We've added a powerful new dashboard that shows you exactly what market conditions the trading bot is detecting in real-time!

**What you can do:**
- See the current market regime (Bull Trending, Bear Trending, Low Vol Range, High Vol Choppy, or Crisis)
- View ML model confidence levels (how sure the bot is about current conditions)
- Track how the bot adjusts strategy allocation based on market conditions
- Review 30 days of regime history to understand market patterns
- Monitor ML model performance and accuracy (91.7%!)

**How to use it:**
1. Click **Regime** in the main navigation menu
2. View the current market regime with color-coded indicator
3. See how strategies are allocated (Bollinger Bounce, RSI Mean Reversion, Breakout)
4. Check ML model accuracy and feature importance
5. Review regime distribution over the last 7 days

**Why this matters:**
The bot automatically adapts its trading strategies based on market conditions. Now you can see exactly what the bot is "thinking" and understand why it's making certain trading decisions. When the market shifts from trending to choppy, you'll see the bot automatically adjust from breakout strategies to mean reversion - all without any manual intervention!

**Learn more:** Visit the [Regime Dashboard Help Page](/help/regime-dashboard) for detailed explanations of each regime type.

## 🎯 Understanding Market Regimes

**🟢 Bull Trending** - Market moving up steadily → Bot uses breakout strategies  
**🔴 Bear Trending** - Market moving down steadily → Bot reduces exposure  
**🔵 Low Vol Range** - Market sideways, low volatility → Bot uses mean reversion  
**🟠 High Vol Choppy** - High volatility, no clear trend → Bot trades cautiously  
**🟣 Crisis** - Extreme volatility → Bot may pause or use very conservative strategies
